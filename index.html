How to Choose Perfect Blackout Curtains

  • Measure correctly: Width should be 2-2.5x window width. Drop from rod to floor or sill.
  • Heading style: Choose eyelet, pencil pleat, wave, or tab top.
  • Pick colors: Neutral tones make rooms bigger, bold colors add drama.
  • Blackout rating: Look for "3-pass" or "triple weave" for 100% darkness.
  • Consider lining: Thermal for insulation, interlining for soundproofing.
  • Functionality: Motorized, cordless (child-safe), or manual?
